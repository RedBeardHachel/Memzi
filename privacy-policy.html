Privacy Policy for Memzi

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Memzi is an English to Hebrew learning application. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled.

1. Information Collection

Memzi is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not collect personal identification information from users unless explicitly provided (e.g., if you contact support).

2. App Functionality and Data

The app uses AI mnemonics and spaced repetition (based on the SM-2 algorithm) to help you learn. Your learning progress, vocabulary, and data are stored locally on your device.

3. Image Recognition

The app may use image recognition features. Any image processing is typically performed locally or through secure AI services, and we do not store your images on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties, except as required for technical functionality of the app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
