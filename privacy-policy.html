1. Introduction

Memzi ("we", "our", "the app") is a language-learning application for Android and Google Chrome that helps users learn Hebrew vocabulary through spaced repetition and AI-generated mnemonic images. Your privacy is important to us.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name to enable cloud sync across devices.
  • Word Cards: The English words and Hebrew translations you save are stored locally on your device and, if you sign in, synced to Google Firebase Firestore under your account.
  • Mnemonic Images: AI-generated images are stored locally on your device only. Image prompts (text descriptions) may be synced to your cloud account.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not record or store the content of your screen.
  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not share, sell, or trade your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Accessibility Service & Overlay

Memzi uses an Accessibility Service solely to detect text selection in other apps and display a translation overlay. This service:

  • Only activates when you explicitly select text in an enabled app.
  • Does not log, store, or transmit the content you are viewing.
  • Does not run in the background when disabled.
  • Can be disabled at any time from Settings > Accessibility.

5. Chrome Extension

The Memzi Chrome extension:

  • Requires the "activeTab" and "scripting" permissions to display translation overlays on web pages when you right-click selected text.
  • Uses the "identity" permission for Google Sign-In to sync your word cards.
  • Does not collect or transmit any browsing data.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Firestore database, Crashlytics. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • MyMemory Translation API: Words are sent to MyMemory for translation. Subject to MyMemory's Terms.
  • Cloudflare Workers AI: Image prompts (text only) are sent to Cloudflare for image generation. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini AI: Word pairs are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate mnemonic captions. Subject to Google AI Terms.

7. Data Retention

Your word cards and account data are retained as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual word cards from the app.
  • Signing out and clearing app data from Android Settings.
  • Contacting us to request full account deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Memzi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
